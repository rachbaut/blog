About

Hello, my name is Rachel and I currently reside in the beautiful city of Irvine, CA. I started this blog in order to share primarily my baking ideas, but I decided to expand it to food in general as well. I love looking up different recipes and try creating them for myself. I have my family try out my creations and let me know what it is I need to improve on. I love baking the most though, why? I love making a cake, bundt cake, donut, etc look beautiful but also I want it to taste as good as it looks. That was a challenge for myself because I have often tasted cute cookies that are rock solid with no flavor. So if you guys have any advice on best techniques to make delicious tasting food I am ALL ears. Now let's get cooking!
